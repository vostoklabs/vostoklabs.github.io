## Description (paste into MW)

**Make a personalized name keychain in seconds — your name, your font, your colours.**

Type a name, pick a font, hit Customize. Great for gifts, party favours, bag tags, backpack charms, and market/Etsy sellers who want a fast, consistent product.

**✨ What you can change**
- **30+ fonts** — bold & bubbly, elegant script, and a full set of **pixel/retro** fonts. Or type any font name in the custom field.
- **Multi-language** — Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Cyrillic/Russian, Hebrew and Arabic font sets built in.
- **Horizontal or vertical** layout (vertical stacks the letters under the ring).
- **1, 2, or 3 colours** — a plate, a coloured outline halo, and the letters, each on its own layer.
- **Raised or engraved** letters, adjustable size & thickness, rounded or contoured plate (`uniform height` gives every name the same tag height — perfect for a seller's rack).
- **Movable keyring loop** — slide it anywhere; it always stays fused to the plate.

**🎨 Two-colour & three-colour printing — with or without AMS**
The plate, halo, and letters each sit on their own layer height, so you get clean multicolour either way:
- **With AMS:** each part is already assigned its own colour — just print.
- **No AMS (any printer):** print in one colour and add a filament change / pause at the layer where the colour should switch. With the default 2.0 mm base and 0.8 mm halo that's a pause at **2.0 mm** (plate → halo) and **2.8 mm** (halo → letters) — at 0.2 mm layers, that's layer **11** and layer **15**.

**🖨️ Print notes**
- No supports, no AMS required. PLA/PETG. A dark plate with light letters gives the best contrast.
